About

As a student, making meals is quite the difficult task. You might never have enough ingredients at home, or you just can't be bothered to cook something on your own. I get it! And it's why I'm here to help. This website was created as a means to share simple recipes that can be done using minimal and simple ingredients. Gone are the days where you FINALLY have ingredients at home, but no ideas of what to do with them. By submitting the form, you gain access to all my recipes, my brand recommendations and nutrient information!
