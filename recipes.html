Overnight Oats

Ingredients:

  • 50g oats
  • 150ml protein chocolate milk or plain milk
  • 80g vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 banana
  • Cinnamon

Instructions:

  • Add the oats and milk to a bowl and mix thoroughly.
  • Cover and add to the fridge overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  • Top with yogurt, chopped bananas, cinnamon, and enjoy!

Tofu Ramen

Ingredients:

  • 100g noodles (udon recommended)
  • Soy sauce
  • 500ml water
  • 1 chicken cube
  • 100g tofu
  • 1 boiled egg
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, ginger powder)
  • Spring onion & sesame

Instructions:

  • Marinate tofu with salt, pepper, and soy sauce for 30 minutes.
  • Cook tofu in a pan with oil, or in an oven or air fryer until it browns.
  • Add water, chicken cube, garlic, and ginger powder to a pot and let it boil.
  • Once it's boiling, add the noodles and cook them for 1-2 minutes.
  • Pour in a bowl and serve with tofu and a boiled egg.
  • Top with spring onion and sesame and enjoy!

Pizza with Yogurt Dough

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  • Add equal parts of yogurt and flour to a bowl, along with baking powder.
  • Mix until a dough starts to form and start kneading with your hands.
  • Flour a surface and flatten dough to desired size.
  • Add your pizza ingredients (sauce, cheese, any toppings).
  • Bake in the oven at 180Â°C for 20 minutes, or until the dough is cooked.
